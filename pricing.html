❓ FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Is there a free trial?
Yes! Every plan starts with a 30-day free trial. No credit card required. You get access to all features of your chosen plan during the trial.
How is the student count calculated?
We count active enrolled students at the end of each month. Inactive, transferred, or graduated students are not counted in your billing.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept bKash, Nagad, Rocket, and all major credit/debit cards. Subscription fees are billed monthly or annually.
Can I switch plans later?
Absolutely. You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Upgrades are prorated immediately. Downgrades take effect at the next billing cycle.
Is my school's data safe and private?
Each school has completely isolated data. No other school can ever see your students, fees, or results. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest.
Do you offer discounts for NGOs or government schools?
Yes, we offer special pricing for government institutions, NGO-run schools, and schools in underprivileged areas. Contact us to discuss.
