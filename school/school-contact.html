❓ FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I apply for admission?
▾
You can apply online using the Admission page, or collect a physical form from our school office. Applications are open from 20 February to 15 March 2025 for Session 2025-26.
How can I pay my child's fees?
▾
Fees can be paid online via the Parent Portal (bKash, Nagad, Rocket, bank transfer), or in person at the school accounts office. You will receive an SMS confirmation after every payment.
How do I check my child's attendance?
▾
Log in to the Parent Portal at dhaka-high.prathomik.edu.bd. You'll see your child's daily attendance, and you will receive an SMS if your child is marked absent.
Where can I see the school's exam schedule?
▾
All exam schedules are published on the Notice Board. You can also download the routine as a PDF from the Downloads section. Students can also see it in the Student Portal.
Is there a scholarship program?
▾
Yes. Students with GPA 5.00 in the half-yearly or annual exam receive a 25% tuition fee waiver for the following semester. Meritorious students from underprivileged backgrounds may apply for full scholarships through the welfare office.
