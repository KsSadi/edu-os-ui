About Dhaka High School

From a small primary school in 1962 to one of Dhaka's most respected institutions — our journey is defined by unwavering commitment to academic excellence and holistic character development.

🎯 Our Purpose

Mission, Vision & Values

🎯
Our Mission
To provide every student with a rigorous, inclusive, and joyful education that equips them with the knowledge, skills, and values to thrive in an ever-changing world and contribute meaningfully to Bangladesh and beyond.
🌟
Our Vision
To be Bangladesh's most trusted school — a place where every child's potential is discovered, developed, and celebrated. We envision a generation of leaders who are intellectually fearless, morally grounded, and socially responsible.
🤝
Our Promise
Every student who walks through our gates deserves a world-class education regardless of background. We promise small class sizes, dedicated teachers, and a safe learning environment where every voice matters.
📜 Our History

Six decades of legacy

What began as a modest primary school has grown into a full-fledged institution serving over 1,200 students across all levels of schooling.

1962
Foundation
Dhaka High School was founded by Mr. Nurul Islam Chowdhury with just 12 students in a two-room building in Dhanmondi.
1975
Secondary Section Added
With growing demand, Classes 6–10 were introduced and the school moved to its current permanent building.
1988
First SSC Batch
Our inaugural SSC batch achieved a remarkable 94% pass rate, earning national recognition.
2002
HSC College Wing
Higher Secondary classes were introduced, making Dhaka High School a complete K-12 institution.
2015
Digital Campus Initiative
Launched smart classrooms, a modern computer lab, and digital attendance systems.
2024
Prathomik EduOS Launch
Became one of the first schools on EduOS — enabling digital fees, results, and parent communication.
