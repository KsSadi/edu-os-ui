Apply for your child's admission to Dhaka High School. Applications are open for Classes I–XII for the 2025-26 academic session. Seats are limited.

How to Apply

Fill Application

  • Birth Certificate (original + photocopy)

  • National ID / Birth Registration of parent/guardian
  • Transfer Certificate (if transferring from another school)
  • Application fee receipt (৳300)
