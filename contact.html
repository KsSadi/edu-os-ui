Frequently asked questions
Is EduOS suitable for government schools? ▼
Yes. EduOS is designed specifically for the Bangladesh education system — including government primary and secondary schools. We follow NCTB curriculum standards and support MPO-listed institutions with tailored pricing.
How does the AI work — do teachers need to be tech-savvy? ▼
Not at all. Our AI features are built to be used by anyone — no technical knowledge needed. A teacher can generate 50 exam questions with one click, or get a student's full progress report written automatically. We also provide full onboarding training.
What does the free demo include? ▼
A 30-minute live session where our team walks you through the platform based on your school type. You'll see real features, ask any questions, and get a personalized pricing quote on the spot. Completely free, no commitment required.
Can I reach you on WhatsApp? ▼
Yes! You can WhatsApp us directly at +880 1741-022832. Our team responds Sunday through Thursday, 9 AM to 6 PM BST. For after-hours queries, send a message and we'll reply first thing next business morning.
Is there a plan for rural or low-income schools? ▼
Absolutely. Bridging the urban-rural education gap is central to our mission. We offer special subsidized pricing for rural upazilas, NGO-run schools, and government-affiliated institutions. Contact us and we'll work out a plan that fits your budget.
